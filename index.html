Demystifying Makeup

What we do

We all want the same thing - to make sure our makeup is safe and that the information available to us is transparent. Our goal is to empower you to know more about the makeup you use on a daily basis. By providing information about common ingredients found in makeup, establishing easier access to report problems to the proper agencies, and having resources and education readily available, we hope that you find what you're looking for.
