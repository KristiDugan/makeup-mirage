Mission

We all want the same thing - to make sure our makeup is safe and that the information available to us is transparent. Our goal is to empower you to know more about the makeup you use on a daily basis. By providing information about common ingredients found in makeup, establishing easier access to the proper agencies for reporting problems, and making resources for further educating yourself readily available, we hope that you find what you're looking for. Look around, and let us know what we can do to make this site of value for you.
